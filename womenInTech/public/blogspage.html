Kamelia Aryafar

Chief Algorithms and Analytics Officer

Google

As Chief Algorithms and Analytics Officer Dr. Kamelia Aryafar works with ML/AI engineers and scientists focused on building Google Cloud AI industry solutions. She was previously Executive Vice President, Chief Algorithms Officer, and board member for Overstock.com, Dr. Kamelia Aryafar led the company’s machine learning, data science, data engineering, and analytics functions across the marketing, customer, sourcing, and website verticals. Since joining Overstock in 2017, Dr. Aryafar’s teams have integrated state-of-the-art machine learning and artificial intelligence algorithms across various product teams, including personalization, pricing, ranking, search, recommender systems, marketing, CRM, advertising technologies, email, sourcing and supply chain.

Susane Diane Wojcicki

Chief executive officer of Youtube

Youtube

Susane Diane Wojcicki is the CEO of YouTube. She has been in the tech industry for over 20 years.Wojcicki was involved in the founding of Google, and became Google's first marketing manager in 1999. She later led the company's online advertising business and was put in charge of Google's original video service. After observing the success of YouTube, Wojcicki proposed the acquisition of YouTube by Google in 2006, and has served as CEO of YouTube since 2014.

Raji Arasu

Senior Vice President, Intuit Platform

Intuit Inc

Raji Arasu is an engineering executive with 20+ years experience leading technology transformations for multi-billion-dollar companies. Since 2016, Arasu has led the Intuit Platform team to deliver the technology services platform that powers Intuit’s financial product experiences (TurboTax, QuickBooks and Mint) for customers and partners. Arasu’s diverse 1100+ member global engineering team delivers foundational services that are used by Intuit product development and data science teams, and external partners. Her capacity to build relationships and work cross-functionally to create pivotal technology and drive business transformation earned her Intuit’s prestigious CEO Leadership Award in 2018.

Sherry Ryan

Vice President and CISO

Juniper Networks

Sherry Ryan is Vice President and CISO of Juniper Networks. Sherry is responsible for leading the strategy, design, development, implementation and maintenance of IT Security programs for Juniper worldwide. She is also responsible for establishing and developing strong relationships with internal and external organizations -- such as law enforcement and industry groups -- that impact or have an interest in cybersecurity. Her organization drives the active management of policies, awareness communications, incident response and security technology solutions for Juniper Networks.
